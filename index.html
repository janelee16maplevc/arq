How a session works.

No setup. No instructions. Just walk in.

01

You walk in and
close the door

The room is private, quiet, and designed to feel like a sanctuary. There are no screens to look at, no device to pick up. Just a chair, soft light, and space.

02

You start talking —
or the room does

ARQ begins gently. You can speak freely about anything — a decision you're wrestling with, something that happened, or just how you're feeling right now.

03

ARQ listens
to everything

Not just your words. ARQ reads your tone of voice, your expressions, your body language — understanding what you mean beneath what you say.

04

The room responds
through sound

ARQ's voice fills the space around you — warm, unhurried, and deeply considered. Like a conversation with someone who has your complete attention.

05

You leave feeling
like yourself again

Sessions typically run 30–60 minutes. Most people describe leaving feeling lighter, clearer, and more ready for whatever comes next.
