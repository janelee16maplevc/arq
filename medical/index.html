About ARQ

Mental health care designed for the demands of high performance.

ARQ is a private, AI-powered room built at the intersection of clinical therapy, performance coaching, and evidence-based meditation. It reads your voice, facial expressions, and body language in real time — adapting to you with the depth and nuance of a trained clinician.

Developed in partnership with leading mental health practitioners, ARQ is not an app or a chatbot. It is a structured, intelligent therapeutic environment — designed to be used regularly, as part of a high-performance mental training practice.
